Common Cyber Attacks

Phishing

Fake emails or messages trick users into revealing passwords or banking details.

High Risk

Ransomware

Malware that locks your files and demands payment to restore access.

Critical

DDoS Attack

Floods a server with traffic to crash websites or online services.

Medium Risk

Social Engineering

Manipulating human psychology instead of hacking systems directly.

High Risk

How to Stay Safe

  • Use strong, unique passwords for every account
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • Never click unknown links or attachments
  • Keep your system and software updated
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities
